Why do we need to write our own diaries?

Writing to yourself is an important means to self-expression.
You’ll know yourself better. You’ll express yourself. You’ll work through hard choices. You’ll develop emotional intimacy. You’ll feel better as you put your thoughts and feelings on paper. You’ll build memories on paper. You’ll create new ideas.
